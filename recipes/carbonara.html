Carbonara

Description

This spaghetti carbonara is a super rich "bacon and egg" pasta dish that's great to serve for company. This recipe also makes an unusual brunch offering.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • olive oil
  • bacon
  • onion
  • garlic
  • white wine
  • eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • black pepper
  • parsley

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Place diced bacon in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon olive oil to bacon fat in the skillet.
  4. Return cooked bacon to the skillet; add cooked spaghetti.
  5. Serve warm with chopped parsley sprinkled on top and extra Parmesan cheese at the table.
